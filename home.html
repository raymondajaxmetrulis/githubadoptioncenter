Welcome to Github Adoption Center!

Our Mission

Github Adoption Center is dedicated to saving the lives of homeless animals through rescue, adoption, and education. We are different from other rescue organizations because we never turn away any homeless, abandoned, injured or sick animal in need.

About The Center

Founded in 2017, Github Adoption Center is an nonprofit organization located in downtown Manhattan.

Adoption

Github adoption center has hundreds of animals available for adoption every day. If you are looking to add an awesome dog to your family, here is the right place to find your new furry friend.
